TRON is dedicated to accelerating the decentralization of the internet through blockchain technology and decentralized applications (DApps). Founded by Justin Sun in September 2017, the TRON network has continued to achieve impressive results since the launch of the mainnet in May 2018. In July 2018, TRON also acquired BitTorrent, a pioneer in decentralized services with nearly 100 million monthly active users, and integrated its ecosystem into BitTorrent. In recent years, the TRON network has gained incredible attention, with more than 232 million users on the blockchain and more than 7.7 billion transactions. In addition, TRON has the largest circulating supply of stablecoins in the world, surpassing USDT on Ethereum in April 2021.
